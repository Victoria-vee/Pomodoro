What is Pomodoro?

Pomodoro is website that helps in automating the use of the pomodoro technique.You may be asking What is the pomodoro technique? Pomodoro technique is a time management technique that was developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.It breaks work into time intervals, typically 25 minutes with a 5-10 minutes break. After 4 pomodoro rounds are taken a long break of about 25-30 minutes is taken.

Why Pomodoro.

The goal of pomodoro is to reduce the chances of any internal and external distractions. This makes you to focus more and get you "in the zone" while working. The breaking down of task makes it easier to accomplish the bigger task.
